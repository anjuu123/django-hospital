Our Services

Following Services are provided by our hospital.

Skilled Nursing

Gifted attendants give both present moment and long haul, proficient consideration to patients who require clinical benefits at home that is because of an intense ailment, handicap, long haul medical issue, or terminal disease. The medical caretaker visits the patient's home to survey current wellbeing status and assess home security..

Physical Therapy Services

Licensed therapists examine and treat persons who have physical requirements as a consequence of sickness, injury, disability, or surgery. Therapeutic activities to increase trunk and extremities strength, range of motion, balance, endurance, and overall reconditioning. Patients should get bed mobility and transfer training to allow them to move around their surroundings as effortlessly as feasible. Instruct on how to manage discomfort after surgery or an injury.

Occupational Therapy Services

Services provided by certified therapists who assess and treat clients with self-care issues that are interfering with their well-being. Dressing, grooming, bathing, food preparation, upper body strengthening, fine motor skills training, and assistive device training are some of the activities of daily living that are taught.

Medical Social Services

Licensed medical social workers provide services. Medical social workers with a license give assistance/counseling to patients by addressing their financial, social, and emotional concerns, as well as providing information about community services. Assessment of social and emotional well-being, financial requirements assessment, community resource planning, and home environment assessment

Speech Therapy Services

Services provided by qualified therapists who examine and treat clients who have swallowing or speech problems. Individuals who require speech therapy are frequently, but not always, stroke survivors. Training for swallowing and feeding issues. As a result of a stroke or a head injury, cognitive problems and communication therapies are required. Training in augmentative communication technologies and voice issues.

Home Health Aide Services

Personal care professionals give hands-on patient care in line with the patient's needs and the plan of care. Bathing, oral care, hair care, clothing, toileting, transferring, and so on are all examples of personal hygiene and grooming. Providing modest workout assistance, Maintaining domestic cleanliness, companionship and support, respite care, doing errands, procuring household supplies, serving meals and feeding carers for light cooking, shopping, and medication pick-up At-home health-care assistance
